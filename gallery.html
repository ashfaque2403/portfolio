Music Player-Django

I have successfully developed a feature-rich music player using Django and Python. This music player application not only allows users to create accounts and securely authenticate but also empowers them to mark their favorite songs for easy access. Users can effortlessly search for songs within the extensive library and enjoy a seamless listening experience. Furthermore, I've integrated a robust song-upload functionality that ensures authenticated users can contribute to the growing collection, fostering a collaborative musical community. With a responsive and user-friendly design, this Django-powered music player offers a delightful platform for music enthusiasts to explore, share, and enjoy their favorite tunes..
